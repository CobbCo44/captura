Features

Everything your practice needs

One platform that handles intake, documentation, and patient communication.

Adaptive Pre-Visit Intake

Patients have a structured conversation with the intake assistant before their appointment. It covers chief complaint, symptom timeline, medications, history, and goals. The assistant asks one question at a time, never rushes, and knows when it has enough.

Replaces the PA interview

60-Second Clinical Snapshots

The conversation becomes a structured document: chief complaint, timeline, current protocol, what's been tried, relevant history, and considerations for physician review. Your doctor reads it in under a minute and walks in with a plan.

Decision support, not a diagnosis

Between-Visit Check-ins

Patients log symptoms, side effects, or questions anytime from their portal. Each check-in gets classified by urgency: routine entries are logged, notable changes go to the doctor's digest, and urgent flags get immediate attention.

Nothing falls through the cracks

Full Patient Record

Every visit, every check-in, every lab result, every message. All in one place, searchable, with a complete timeline. Set protocols, add notes, and schedule follow-ups right from the patient's profile.

Longitudinal memory across years

Security

Built for trust

Healthcare data demands the highest standard of care. Here's how CatchCare protects it.

HIPAA-ready architecture

Row-level security on every table. Data scoped to each doctor's panel. No patient can see another patient's data. No doctor can see another doctor's patients.

No data sharing. Ever.

Your patient data is never used to train models. It is never shared with third parties. It is never sold. It stays in your database, period.

Technology that knows its limits

CatchCare never diagnoses, never prescribes, never gives clinical advice. It gathers information and surfaces patterns. Every clinical decision stays with the physician.

Full transparency

Patients see exactly what information was captured. Doctors see exactly what the system generated. Clinical considerations are doctor-facing only, structurally separated at the database level.
