Features

Everything your practice needs

One platform for intake, documentation, and patient communication.

Adaptive Pre-Visit Intake

Patients have a structured conversation before their appointment. Chief complaint, symptom timeline, medications, history, and goals. One question at a time.

Replaces the PA interview
Hi Sarah. I'm here to help your doctor prepare for your visit. What's the main reason you're coming in today?
I've been getting bad headaches for about two weeks. They start behind my eyes and get worse in the afternoon.
Thank you. On a scale of 1 to 10, how would you rate the pain at its worst?

60-Second Clinical Snapshots

The intake conversation becomes a structured clinical document. Your doctor reads it in under a minute and walks in with a plan.

Decision support, not a diagnosis
Chief Complaint
Bilateral frontal headaches x 2 weeks, retro-orbital onset, progressive afternoon worsening
Current Protocol
Ibuprofen 400mg PRN (limited relief), no preventive therapy initiated
Considerations for Review
Screen for tension-type vs. migraine features. Recent screen time increase noted. No red flag symptoms reported.

Between-Visit Check-ins

Patients log symptoms anytime. Each check-in gets classified by urgency. Nothing falls through the cracks.

Triaged by urgency
Headache severity increased to 9/10, new nausea 2h ago
Ibuprofen stopped working after day 3 2 days ago
Started tracking triggers in a journal 4 days ago

Meet Mira

Your clinical
chief of staff

Mira sits on top of your entire patient panel. Instead of clicking through dashboards, just ask.

"Walk me through today" Mira pulls your appointments, flags urgent check-ins, and tells you where to start.
"Tell me about Eric before I see him" Full snapshot, labs, protocol, recent check-ins, and your own notes. In seconds.
"Which patients reported headaches this month?" Mira searches across your entire panel and surfaces matches with context.
"Draft a reply to Sarah about her labs" Mira writes the draft. You review and send. One click.

Mira never gives clinical advice. She surfaces patterns, pulls data, and routes decisions to you. Your judgment, her speed.

Security

Built for trust

Healthcare data demands the highest standard. Here's how CatchCare protects it.

HIPAA-ready architecture

Row-level security on every table. Data scoped to each doctor's panel. No patient can see another patient's data.

No data sharing. Ever.

Patient data is never used to train models, shared with third parties, or sold. It stays in your database, period.

Technology that knows its limits

CatchCare never diagnoses, prescribes, or gives clinical advice. Every clinical decision stays with the physician.

Full transparency

Patients see exactly what was captured. Doctors see exactly what the system generated. Clinical considerations are structurally separated.
