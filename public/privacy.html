CatchCare

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

CatchCare ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal and health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our platform.

Information We Collect

When you use CatchCare, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Email address, name, and date of birth when you create an account or are invited by your doctor.
  • Health information: Symptoms, medical history, medications, supplements, and other health details you share during intake conversations and check-ins.
  • Lab results: Lab values extracted from PDFs you upload.
  • Messages: Communications between you and your doctor through the platform.
  • Usage data: Basic technical information like browser type and access times to keep the platform running smoothly.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the CatchCare service: conducting intake conversations, generating clinical snapshots, facilitating communication with your doctor.
  • To process your health information through AI to create structured summaries for your physician.
  • To notify your doctor of urgent check-ins that may require immediate attention.
  • To improve the platform's functionality and reliability.

How We Protect Your Information

  • Database security: All data is stored in a secure PostgreSQL database with row-level security policies. Each patient can only access their own records. Each doctor can only access their own patients.
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL.
  • Access controls: Server-side functions verify authorization before returning any data.
  • Structural separation: AI-generated clinical considerations are stored separately from patient-visible data and are never shown to patients.

What We Never Do

  • We never sell your data to anyone.
  • We never share your health information with third parties for marketing or advertising.
  • We never use your data to train AI models.
  • We never allow one doctor to see another doctor's patients.
  • We never allow one patient to see another patient's data.

AI and Your Data

CatchCare uses AI (powered by Anthropic's Claude) to conduct intake conversations, generate clinical snapshots, classify check-in urgency, and parse lab results. Your health information is sent to the AI only to perform these specific tasks. The AI does not retain your data between sessions, and your data is not used to train AI models.

The AI never provides clinical advice, diagnoses conditions, or recommends treatments. All clinical decisions are made by your physician.

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active and your doctor maintains an active CatchCare account. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

  • Access your data through your patient portal at any time.
  • Request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for AI processing (though this will limit platform functionality).

HIPAA Compliance

CatchCare is architected to support HIPAA compliance. Before onboarding real patient data, we require signed Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) with all infrastructure providers. Our current deployment uses synthetic and self-reported test data only.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at privacy@catchcare.com.
